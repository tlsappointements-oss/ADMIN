The process

From request to payment
in four steps

01

Submit a Request

Any team member submits a purchase request using a guided smart form in under 2 minutes.

02

Automatic Routing

Procura routes the request to the right approvers based on category, amount, and department rules.

03

Approve & Issue PO

Approvers review and sign off in one click. A purchase order is generated and sent to the supplier automatically.

04

Receive & Pay

Match invoices to POs, verify delivery, and trigger payment — all inside Procura with a full audit trail.
